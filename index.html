Help & FAQs

How does delivery work across Australia?

We ship Australia-wide using freight partners suited to each product. You'll get tracking, a delivery window, and instructions on access requirements before dispatch.

What's your returns policy?

Change-of-mind returns are accepted within 30 days on most items in original condition. Faulty or damaged goods are covered immediately — just contact our team with a photo.

Can I see a product in person before I buy?

We offer free fabric and tile samples, detailed spec sheets, and video walkarounds. For large items, our team can arrange a showroom visit with one of our trade partners.

How do I contact customer service?

Call 1300 123 456 Mon–Sat or email hello@thebluespace.com.au. We aim to respond within two business hours.
